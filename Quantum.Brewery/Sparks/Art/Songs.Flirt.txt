**"Firebird"**  
*(A whisper, then a spark)*  

You flicker in the twilight,  
A glance — and I ignite.  
No match, no flame, no warning,  
Just you… and sudden light.  

Wings of ember, breath so bold,  
Dancing close, then taking flight.  
A tease of heat, a story told  
In shadows leaping, fierce and bright.  

Come closer, love, don’t turn away —  
This spark won’t fade, it *pulls* me in.  
One kiss, and we’re both lost in flame,  
Two hearts alight, where do we begin?  

So burn, my bird, and set me free —  
This fire’s fate, this wild desire.  
You’re not just flame… you’re *everything*.  
You’re the spark, the smoke, the fire.  

---  

The firebird symbolizes **instant passion** ("ignite"), **flirtatious dance** ("tease of heat"), and **inescapable attraction** ("pulls me in").

## 🎶 **"Rematch"**

The rack’s lifted, the chalk’s still warm,  
You’re back at the table—*playing me like a storm*.  
Same corner light, same crooked grin,  
But now I know *how deep this game goes in*.  

You break slow—*a tease in motion*,  
I catch your breath in the smoky commotion.  
That cue glides like a whispered vow,  
And I’m already down… *before the eight ball drops now*.  

*You’ve got me leaning where I shouldn’t lean,*  
*Fingers brushing the felt like a dirty dream.*  
Is it skill? Or the way your thigh *grazes mine*?  
One near-miss kiss—*and the game’s intertwined*.  

No rules called, no warnings given,  
We’re not just playing… we’re *forgiving*.  
The last loss? Just a prelude, it seems—  
A warm-up for *these tangled limbs and dreams*.  

So take your shot, love, but don’t pretend  
This is just chalk, wood, and a winning end.  
The pockets run deep, but so does this glow…  
The table remembers—*and so do we, slow*.  

Rack it again? I’ll never say no.  
‘Cause baby, we’re not playing for points…  
*We’re playing for soul*.
