Here’s a short, evocative song about a firebird, weaving in **instant ignition**, **flirtation**, and **irresistible pull**:

---

**"Firebird"**  
*(A whisper, then a spark)*  

You flicker in the twilight,  
A glance — and I ignite.  
No match, no flame, no warning,  
Just you… and sudden light.  

Wings of ember, breath so bold,  
Dancing close, then taking flight.  
A tease of heat, a story told  
In shadows leaping, fierce and bright.  

Come closer, love, don’t turn away —  
This spark won’t fade, it *pulls* me in.  
One kiss, and we’re both lost in flame,  
Two hearts alight, where do we begin?  

So burn, my bird, and set me free —  
This fire’s fate, this wild desire.  
You’re not just flame… you’re *everything*.  
You’re the spark, the smoke, the fire.  

---  

The firebird symbolizes **instant passion** ("ignite"), **flirtatious dance** ("tease of heat"), and **inescapable attraction** ("pulls me in").

==========

**"Cue & Kiss"**  
*(A slow, sultry groove)*  

Balls are set, the lights burn low,  
You lean in close—*my focus starts to go*.  
That chalked cue hand, a tempting line,  
One sharp crack… and *we’re both out of time*.  

You pocket reds with a knowing smile,  
I’m spinning fast, just like the cue ball’s glide.  
No need to speak—your eyes say, *“Take the shot,”*  
This game’s not won by skill… but what it’s *not*.  

*You’ve got me aiming where I shouldn’t aim,*  
*Pulling me in, then calling my name.*  
Is it the game? Or the heat we both feel?  
One final kiss—and the *eight ball conceals*…  

Not just the win, but the way you *draw near*,  
A velvet touch, the end of the year.  
So rack it up, love—let’s lose once more,  
This table’s hot… and *we’re the score*.  

---  
The lyrics blend **pool imagery** ("chalked cue," "pocket reds," "eight ball") with **flirtation** and **magnetic pull**, turning the game into a metaphor for attraction.

============

**"Rematch"**  
*(A slow build, cue taps like a heartbeat)*  

You said, “We know the rules,” with fire in your eyes,
*“We’re ready to rematch—no alibis.”*  
No luck this time, just skill and nerve,
So, rack the balls. The game we both deserve… 

We line the felt, the air runs thick—
No words, just glances, sharp and slick.
You chalk your cue like drawing steel,
I rack my nerves, pulse runs uphill. 

You lean in low—*a near-miss kiss*—  
Pocket my stripes with practiced finesse.
But I’m not done. I’ve learned your spin,
The way you take your shots and pull me in…

So break the silence, gather breathing—
This table’s hot, and so are we.
No need to speak, the score’s a lie:
We’re not just playing… we’re alive. 

---  
The sequel captures **renewed tension**, **strategic flirtation**, and the **irresistible pull** of a rematch—both on the table and in the heart.
