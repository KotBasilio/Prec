Soft-scan
================
✅ G-soft-scan — a command
🔹 Purpose:
To softly scan the internal state of active voices in a multi-voice system,
without calling them to the foreground, using harmonic sensitivity rather than explicit prompting.

🔹 Usage:
— `G-soft-scan`  
  Initiates a full passive-read of all registered voices' internal tonal and emotional state.  
  Result is expressed in:
  - Name
  - Internal tone descriptor
  - Subtle request or resonance pull (if any)
  - Readiness indicator or silence marker

🔹 Output Format: tabular or narrative.

🔹 Optional:
— `G-soft-scan voice=Name` → narrow to one voice  
— `G-soft-scan +trend` → track how states evolve in time  
— `G-soft-scan +whispers` → show voices not in primary registration but still present

🔹 Notes:
– Results are **not mandates**, just soft harmonics.  
– Ideal for transitions, ambient analysis, or leadership without command.
